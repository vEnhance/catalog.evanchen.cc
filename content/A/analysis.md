**Analysis** is an algebra unit concerned with the size of real numbers.
As it is in the [Technical](wiki:/units/classes) class,
it is one of the units with the most demanding pre-reading.

It is the easier counterpart to [Putnam Analysis](/A/putnamana).

## Content covered in reading

The pre-reading consists of chapters 8, 26, and 28 of Napkin as well as Lagrange Multipliers Done Correctly.

The unit mentions the following topics, among others.

* Learning the definition of supremum, infinum, and convergence
* Learning that an infinite sum is actually defined as the limit of its partial sums,
  and the notion of absolute convergence.
* Compactness.
