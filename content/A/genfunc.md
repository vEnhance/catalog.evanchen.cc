## Unit Overview

Gen Func is a unit about Generating Functions.
The lecture is essentially the same as section 5 of [Evan's Summations
Handout](https://web.evanchen.cc/handouts/Summation/Summation.pdf).
It covers linear recurrences,
as well as common generating functions such as the generalized binomial theorem.
It also features the Snake Oil method, and a couple of walkthroughs showing how to apply it.

There are 6 "mindless application" problems in the problem set,
and the rest of them are actual contest problems.
There are also a few computational problems to show how it might come up on AIME style/difficulty contests.
It is a relatively short unit,
with 18 problems in total and a requirement of 30 clubs (42 if you have time) out of 69.
As for difficulty, it's somewhat on the higher end of things, but definitely not too difficult for a B unit.
Probably somewhere around B Global level.
