## Summary
Inequalities that can be solved using standard functional techniques, Jensen, Karamata, n-1 EV, etc. Utilizes section 2 of Evan's Inequalities handout for reading. A shorter unit with low club requirement and club values.

## Notable Problems
02MOP, a 5 club required problem.

[97JPN](https://aops.com/community/p537), a classical application of the Tangent Line Trick.

[16SLA1](https://aops.com/community/p8639254), a notorious inequality known for being very difficult for an A1.
