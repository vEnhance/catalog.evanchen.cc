### Content and Brief Overview

The theoretical portion of this unit consists of mainly <span class="math math-inline">2</span> big results. The famous result of Turan's Theorem and one of it's corollaries: Mantel's lemma. Having said that, it is advised that the reader doesn't get to worked up with the 2 above ideas and on the contrary still looks out for equality cases and exploit all opportunities to carry out induction.

### Pre-requisites

 Readers should be comfortable with ideas of induction and analyzing equality cases. Introduction to Graph Theoretical terminologies is not in the scope of this unit, so the reader should get acquainted with the same if they're not fluent with them already.
