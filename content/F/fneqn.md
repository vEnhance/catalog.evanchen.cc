## Unit Summary

This unit is all about exploring a staple of olympiad algebra, being functional equations. The unit teaches basic substitution strategies, injectivity, surjectivity, pointwise trap, Cauchy's functional equation, amongst other strategies you will encounter while attempting the problems. The problems are quite varied, with several real, integer, positive real, polynomial FEs. 

The unit is pretty easy for a D unit, although there are certainly some challenging problems. After taking this unit, other good FE units include Monster FE and Wrapped FE.

The unit has 71 clubs across 20 questions in total, not counting the multiplier.
