This is a bit of a mixture of all the "fundamental" number theory topics related to Diophantine equations:
orders modulo a prime, polynomial equations, and quadratic reciprocity.
Its content is essentially all from [Expon
NT](https://otis.evanchen.cc/wiki/units/list-of-number-theory-units/expon-nt/)
and [Heavy NT](https://otis.evanchen.cc/wiki/units/list-of-number-theory-units/heavy-nt/),
and as one might expect its problem set also has substantial overlap with them,
as well as some problems related to the [Size in
NT](https://otis.evanchen.cc/wiki/units/list-of-number-theory-units/size-in-nt/)
and [Euclidean Algorithm](https://otis.evanchen.cc/wiki/units/list-of-number-theory-units/euclid-alg/) units.

It is one of the hardest NT units,
though not quite as challenging as [N7
Summit](https://otis.evanchen.cc/wiki/units/list-of-number-theory-units/n7-summit/).
The unit contains extremely difficult questions such as [this diophantine
equation](https://aops.com/community/c6h505301p3028616) from the expon section
and [ELMO 2013/3](https://aops.com/community/p3104308) from the size/euclid section.
