**Hyperbola** is about hyperbolas, as you'd expect.
In particular, rectangular circumhyperbolas are studied, as well as a little bit of isogonal conjugation.
It is recommended you know several classical orthocenter and incenter
configurations as well as having decent projective geo base (so maybe homography could be a prereq).
Some useful results will be key in making otherwise scary looking problems much simpler.

## Notable Problems

* [Fontené's three theorems](https://mathworld.wolfram.com/FonteneTheorems.html):
  three results about pedal circles, isogonal conjugates, and the nine-point circle.
* [TSTST 2020/6](https://aops.com/community/p19444197):
  converse of anticenter configuration but not bad for a P6.
