## Unit Summary

This unit is a good introduction to olympiad geometry, covering the same topics as Chapters 1-3 in EGMO, being angle chasing, power of a point, and homothety. The unit teaches how to use these synthetic tactics to their maximum potential, teaching the user to master their fundamentals. The problems are highly synthetic, often relying on making slick observations that show one point is equal to another. A good diagram is often helpful. 

This unit is about medium for a B unit and an easy to medium D unit, although some problems are quite challenging for those who are not experienced with geometry.

After this unit, one can look forward to other standard geometry units, such as [Bary](https://otis.evanchen.cc/wiki/units/list-of-geometry-units/bary/), [Classical Geo](https://otis.evanchen.cc/wiki/units/list-of-geometry-units/classical-geo/), [Harmonic](https://otis.evanchen.cc/wiki/units/list-of-geometry-units/harmonic/), [Complex](https://otis.evanchen.cc/wiki/units/list-of-geometry-units/complex-nums/), amongst others.
